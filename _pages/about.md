I am an experienced academic researcher, teacher, and a leader who is passionate about using cutting-edge technologies to understand and unlock the secrets of the human brain. My expertise in biomedical signal processing, machine/deep learning, and data science has enabled me to pioneer innovative methods for diagnosing neurological disorders and identifying biomarkers from EEG signals. My research extends to developing automated systems for biomarker discovery for neurological diseases, analyzing epilepsy presurgical evaluations and surgical outcomes, and advancing brain-computer interfaces with a focus on improving patient care and revolutionizing healthcare systems. I am also an advocate of open science principles, fostering transparency, collaboration, and accessibility in research.
