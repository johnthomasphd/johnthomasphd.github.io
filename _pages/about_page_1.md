# Brain Disorders

This theme explores various neurological disorders, focusing on developing diagnostic tools, improving surgical outcomes, and understanding underlying pathologies. Key areas include epilepsy, autism, and innovative stimulation techniques.

## Epilepsy Diagnosis

*Research focused on enhancing epilepsy diagnosis using EEG data and machine learning algorithms.*

2. **J. Thomas, J. Jin, P. Thangavel, et al.**, “Automated detection of interictal epileptiform discharges from scalp electroencephalograms by convolutional neural networks,” *International Journal of Neural Systems*, vol. 30, no. 11, p. 2050030, 2020. ## Epilepsy Surgery

*This area focuses on improving surgical strategies for epilepsy, including identifying epileptogenic zones and optimizing patient outcomes.*

1. **J. Thomas, C. Abdallah, Z. Cai, et al.**, “Investigating current clinical opinions in stereoelectroencephalography-informed epilepsy surgery,” *Epilepsia*, 2024.
2. **J. Thomas, C. Abdallah, K. Jaber, V. Latreille, L. Minotti, et al.**, “Development of a seizure matching system for clinical decision-making in epilepsy surgery,” *Journal of Neural Engineering*, in press, 2024.
