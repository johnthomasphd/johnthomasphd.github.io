# Healthy Brain

This theme focuses on understanding the functioning of a healthy brain, exploring sleep patterns, brain-computer interfaces, and cognitive processes to gain insights into human cognition and behavior.

## Sleep

*Investigating sleep patterns and their effects on brain health and neurological conditions, particularly in relation to epilepsy.*

- **S. Hannan, J. Thomas, K. Jaber, et al.** (2024). *The differing effects of sleep on ictal and interictal network dynamics in drug-resistant epilepsy*. Annals of Neurology, 95(1), 42–56.

- **A. Ho, S. Hannan, J. Thomas, et al.** (2023). *Rapid eye movement sleep affects interictal epileptic activity differently in mesiotemporal and neocortical areas*. Epilepsia, 64(11), 3036–3048.

- **V. Latreille, T. Avigdor, J. Thomas, et al.** (2023). *Scalp and hippocampal sleep correlates of memory function in drug-resistant temporal lobe epilepsy*. Sleep, zsad228.

## Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI)

*Developing and optimizing interfaces for direct communication between the brain and external devices, improving neuroprosthetics, communication aids, and biometric systems.*

- **R. Yuvaraj, P. Thangavel, J. Thomas, J. Fogarty, and F. Ali** (2023). *Comprehensive analysis of feature extraction methods for emotion recognition from multichannel EEG recordings*. Sensors, 23(2), 915. [https://doi.org/10.3390/s23020915](https://doi.org/10.3390/s23020915)

- **D. Jeswani, P. Kumar Govarthan, A. Selvaraj, C. Bobby Thomas, J. Thomas, and J. F. Agastinose Ronickom** (2023). *A feasibility study on using EEG for biometric trait authentication system*. Current Directions in Biomedical Engineering, 9, 690–693.

- **J. Jeswani, ..., J. Thomas, et al.** (2023). *Low valence low arousal stimuli: An effective candidate for EEG-based biometrics authentication system*. Caring is Sharing–Exploiting the Value in Data for Health and Innovation, IOS Press, 257–261.

- **J. Thomas, T. Maszczyk, N. Sinha, T. Kluge, and J. Dauwels** (2017). *Deep learning-based classification for brain-computer interfaces*. In 2017 IEEE International Conference on Systems, Man, and Cybernetics (SMC), IEEE, 234–239.

## Memory/Cognition

*Exploring the neural mechanisms underlying memory and cognitive functions to better understand learning and information retention.*

- **V. Latreille, J. a. Corbin-Lapointe, J. Thomas, et al.** (2024). *Oscillatory and non-oscillatory sleep electroencephalography biomarkers of the epileptic network*. Epilepsia, in press.
