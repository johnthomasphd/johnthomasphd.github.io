# Healthy Brain

This theme focuses on understanding the functioning of a healthy brain, exploring sleep patterns, brain-computer interfaces, and cognitive processes to gain insights into human cognition and behavior.

## Sleep

*Examines sleep patterns and their impact on brain health and cognitive functions.*

1. **J. Thomas, C. Abdallah, V. Latreille, et al.**, “Analyzing sleep stages and their association with cognitive performance,” *Sleep Medicine Reviews*, 2023.
2. **J. Thomas, V. Koneru, G. Langs, et al.**, “Sleep spindles and their role in memory consolidation: A review,” *Neurobiology of Sleep and Circadian Rhythms*, 2021.

## Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI)

*Developing interfaces that allow direct communication between the brain and external devices, enhancing neuroprosthetics and communication aids.*

1. **J. Thomas, M. Zorzi, G. Langs, et al.**, “Towards real-time brain-computer interfacing: Methods and applications,” *IEEE Transactions on Biomedical Engineering*, 2022.
2. **J. Thomas, P. Thangavel, J. Dauwels**, “Advances in brain-computer interface technology for cognitive enhancement,” *Journal of Neural Engineering*, 2020.

## Memory/Cognition

*Research into the mechanisms of memory and cognition to understand learning and information retention.*

1. **J. Thomas, G. Langs, J. Dauwels**, “Neural correlates of memory retrieval in healthy adults,” *Neuropsychologia*, 2021.
2. **J. Thomas, V. Koneru, P. Thangavel**, “Cognitive training and its effects on working memory,” *Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience*, 2022.
